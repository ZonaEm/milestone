Hi! I'm Red

I love to visually
design, create, & express ideas

I am, by trade, a licensed and board certified behavior analyst in sunny Arizona.

When I was a kid, I had dreams of becoming a graphic designer some day. Then I had a kid with autism and my trajectory changed for a bit.

While I analyzed, taught, managed, and directed (like a boss does), I realized the visual and creative arts side of me was being severely neglected!

I look forward to making your visions a reality, and fulfilling one of my lifelong passions.

Let's make amazing, bright, beautiful, expressive things together!
